adidas World Cup Campaign

Adidas wanted to make a bold splash for the Qatar World Cup, aiming to stand out in the competitive football market. They turned to us for expertise in crafting an unforgettable event, one that would rival the impressive spectacles Nike has been known for. We really wanted to elevate Adidas' presence in Australian football and showcase their striking World Cup shirt designs.

Goals

The primary objective was to reignite Adidas' market share in Australian football, where their influence had waned. Additionally, we aimed to spotlight their bold World Cup shirt designs, positioning them as must-have items for fans and fashion enthusiasts alike. This was about more than just sales; it was about reinvigorating the brand's image and inspiring a sense of excitement around Adidas' offerings.

Action

To achieve these goals, we conceptualized a unique fashion-style runway event. Recognizing the growing synergy between fashion and football, we knew this approach would captivate audiences. By pairing Adidas' innovative football jerseys with their high-end lifestyle streetwear, we created an evening that celebrated both football culture and style. The event was designed to be a sensory experience, combining the dynamic energy of sport with the elegance of fashion.

Results

The big event was a resounding success, garnering international recognition through a feature in Soccerbible. Adidas was thrilled with the outcome, which not only boosted their brand image but also paved the way for future collaborations. The impact was tangible: all key football shirts sold out in the lead-up to the World Cup, confirming that our strategy was both effective and engaging. This project underscored our ability to blend creativity with strategic insight, delivering results that resonate with fans and elevate brand profiles.
